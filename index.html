Hello! I am Biswajit Das

I have 9+ years of extensive experience in delivering proven results through the management of technical operations and projects, within time and budget constraints including 6+ years in web design and development.
I became Certified Scrum Master (2017), Certified SAFe Agilist (2018), Certified Agile Coach (2019), and finally unlocked Professional Scrum Master (2020), Professional Product Owner (2020) and Scrum Advanced Professional (2020).
Please don't hold those against me as I have been practicing Agile methodologies since 2014.
Fast Forward to 2017. This is when I was officially bitten by the Agile bug and started as Scrum Master in my organization that is going through an Agile Transformation. I quickly learned that something wasn't quite right as we weren't getting the results we expected. I decided to get formal training, joined the Agile community and the rest is a fantastic journey. I found my true passion and helped repair a broken implementation of Scrum.
Since then I have become more deeply involved in the Agile community, have spoken at several local meetups and conference. I have participated in discussion groups and weekly Agile coaching round-tables many times. I have worked with software teams in various capacities from facilitating training and workshops to getting deeply involved ‘in the trenches’ with teams. This includes numerous coaching responsibilities from mentoring new teams to training newly converted Scrum Masters to providing technical guidance on Agile practices.
